Infrastructure


Auditorium

The school has a spacious air-conditioned auditorium and a multi-purpose hall with high-tech acoustic and light system. The auditorium is used to host cultural programmes such as Founder’s Day celebrations, Prerna Diwas (Chairperson birthday celebrations), prize distribution ceremonies, orientation programmes, farewell programmes and class presentations. Multipurpose hall is being used to host exhibitions, talks, presentations and meetings.

Sports

We firmly believe in the Perfect Health Programme. Therefore, due importance is given to physical wellbeing. We strongly propound a vigorous sports programme in the campus. Sports is an essential part of the school curriculum, as ‘a sound mind is in a sound body’. The school has a large playground, with lush grass, used for Football, Cricket, Archery, Track and field events and Kho-Kho. Besides this, there are hard courts for Badminton, Basketball, Skating, Kabaddi and Tennis. The dedicated trainers and sports faculty of the school strive to train students for various activities through hard-work, zeal, team-work, resilience and sportsmanship.

Computer Lab

Students are provided with well- equipped Computer labs. The students get hands-on experience with the latest technology and software. LCD projects are used for the demonstration, which assist students to have a better comprehension of the concept.

General Science Lab

The students of Middle and Secondary school perform hands-on experiments related to everyday Science. Students get an opportunity to know the unknown. The lab is well ventilated with a lot of natural light.

Home Science Lab

Home Science Lab is well equipped with all modern equipments where students learn to cook, serve and present various cuisines. Time to time various competitions are organized to showcase their talents.

Library

The school library is a welcoming place for study and reading. The library encourages the love of reading and development of student’s independent learning skills. The library has both printed as well as audio-visual material like CD-ROM and DVDS. There are plenty of books, encyclopedias, picture books, activity books, dictionaries. We have a Junior Reading Room where the young budding Amitians are exposed to good reading habits thus leading them to become voracious readers. Reading room is equipped with a Kyan where children’s phonetic is enhanced and vocabulary development is personalized.

Biology Lab

The Biology Laboratory is well equipped with latest instruments like projection microscopes, microscopes, slides, stains, forceps and needles required for performing experiments. There are also plants/animal specimens with lots of 3D models and charts.

Health Clinic

A spacious, well lit, air-conditioned health clinic with stretchers and updated medicines with other facilities for emergencies. Qualified doctor and senior nursing officer on duty. 24*7 Ambulance is available for emergencies.




Achievements

Gold medal at INSEF National and Regional

Vrinda Anand (XI A) bagged gold medal at INSEF National and Regional Fair and will now represent India at Global Youth Science and Technology Bowl( Hong Kong). She has also qualified for Genius Olympiad(USA).

Archery Competitions

Sanchita Tiwari won many medals and accolades in different Archery competitions during the session 2016 -17.On the basis of her performance, she applied for the scheme of Center of Excellency run by Sports Authority of India for International Events and she was the youngest student to be selected for the above scheme. She will be trained for one full year and depending upon her performance she can receive free trainings till the age of 25 years.

Ramanujan

School team bagged the second position in 'Math Quiz' for Class VI-VIII and the third position in ‘Problem solving' for Class IX-X in the 19th edition of Ramanujan. The annual inter school competition of math was hosted by AIS Noida and more than 250 students from 29 schools of Delhi / NCR participated in it.
